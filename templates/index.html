The Cube is Dukeâ€™s student-run, entrepreneurial residential community located at 205 Oregon Street. We yearn to make a difference, are driven to execute upon and deliver great ideas and are dedicated to learning and growing.


Entrepreneur Community

We are a community of economists, business developers, engineers, computer scientists, and more. We support each other whether that be through technical help for a new startup idea or simply advice on how to approach implementation.


Resources

Duke holds a startup challenges and has the Melissa & Dough Entrepreneurship program, both of which a number of Cube members have participated.


Mentorship

The Cube has an extensive alumni network that have advised undergraduate Cube members in building their own companies.
